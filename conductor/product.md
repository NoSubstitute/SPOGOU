# Product Definition - SPOGOU

## Initial Concept
SPOGOU (Set Passwords On a Group Of Users) is an easy-to-use Google Workspace add-on designed to set passwords for many users (such as a class or an entire school year) based on a Google Workspace group address and share the results back to the requester.

## Target Audience
- Teachers with appropriate admin rights
- School administrators
- IT staff

## Goals
- **Simplify Bulk Resets:** Streamline the process of resetting passwords for specific student groups or classes.
- **Secure Sharing:** Provide a secure and easy way to share new credentials with the teachers who need them.
- **Delegate Workload:** Reduce the burden on central IT departments by safely delegating password reset capabilities to authorized school personnel.

## Key Features
- **Group Membership Retrieval:** Fetch members from a specified Google Workspace group.
- **Automated Password Management:** Generate and apply new passwords to all members of the group.
- **Transparent Reporting:** Generate a report (e.g., in a spreadsheet) containing the new credentials and share it automatically with the requester.
